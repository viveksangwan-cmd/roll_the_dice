Game Rules 😍

  • Player have 2 options.
    • Roll a dice.
    • Hold a dice.
  • After Rolling a dice , value to dice become 1.Current score become 0 and chance will be given to next player.
  • If the value to dice is not 1 , value will be added to CURRENT SCORE.
  • If Player HOLDS the dice. Current Score will be added to main score and chance will be given to next player.
  • Player who scores 100 first , WINS.🎉
